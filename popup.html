      Epoch Explorer
      Unix time counts seconds since January 1, 1970, 00:00:00 UTC (the Unix Epoch). Timestamps in seconds fit in a 32-bit integer until January 19, 2038 (the Y2K38 problem). JavaScript uses milliseconds by default.
      ⚠️
      Year 2038 Problem
      32-bit signed integers overflow at 2,147,483,647 — which corresponds to 03:14:07 UTC on January 19, 2038.

      Precision Guide
      10 digits = seconds | 13 digits = milliseconds | 16 digits = microseconds | 19 digits = nanoseconds
